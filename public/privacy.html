Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts, we collect:

  • Your TikTok display name and avatar (via user.info.basic scope)
  • Your follower counts across connected platforms (via user.info.stats scope)
  • Your published video metadata and metrics: views, likes, comments, shares (via video.list scope)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to display analytics in the EIZ Metrics dashboard. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored separately from public metrics data.

4. Data Retention

Metrics data is retained as time-series data to show growth trends. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Vercel — application hosting
  • Meta Graph API — Instagram metrics
  • YouTube Data API v3 — YouTube metrics
  • TikTok API v2 — TikTok metrics

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To disconnect your TikTok account, you can revoke access from your TikTok settings.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
